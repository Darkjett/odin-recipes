Toast Pizza

Description

It is a very simple recipe you can make for a quick snack and the crunch of the bread is the best part of it

Ingredients

  • A slice of a bread
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese
  • Seasoning

How to make it

  1. Take a nonstick pan and toast just one side of the bread
  2. On the toasted side of the bread put the pizza sauce and cheese if you like you can put seasoning
  3. Now take the bread and put it in the pan on low flame, put the lid of the pan and let it do the job
  4. Check after some minutes if the cheese has melted and the bottom of the bread has a nice crust after that enjoy your Toast Pizza
