Potato Sandwich

Description

This is just a simple potato sandwich you can make this with just 2 to 4 ingredients

Ingredients

  • Sandwich Bread
  • Boiled Potato
  • Cheese
  • Sandwich Seasoning

How to make it

  1. Take the slice's of the bread and put slice's of the boiled potato on the one of the bread slice
  2. Put cheese on the top of the potato and if you have any sandwich seasoning put it on the cheese
  3. Now just put the other slice up and that's it Enjoy Your Sandwich
